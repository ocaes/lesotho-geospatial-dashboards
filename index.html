Lesotho Geospatial Dashboards Portal

A centralized access point for national land, soil, biodiversity, and climate information systems

Welcome

This portal provides unified access to key geospatial dashboards supporting land management, soil assessment, biodiversity monitoring, and climate-related decision-making in the Kingdom of Lesotho. The dashboards are developed by national institutions and partners and open in separate tabs for full functionality and performance.

Use the thematic menu on the left to explore available tools. Additional dashboards will be added as they become available.

  • LAND
  • Lesotho Land Cover Dashboard
  • ROLL Field Dashboard
  • SOIL
  • Lesotho Soil Information System
  • BIODIVERSITY
  • Rangeland Health Dashboard
  • Birds App – Lesotho Avifauna
  • Rangeland Species Searcher
  • CLIMATE & ENVIRONMENT
  • Pollution Monitor – Emissions
  • Fire Monitor – Fire Activity
