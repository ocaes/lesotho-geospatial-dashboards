Welcome

This portal provides unified access to key geospatial dashboards that support land management, soil assessment, biodiversity monitoring, and climate-related analysis in Lesotho. It serves as a centralized gateway to spatial information systems developed by national institutions and partners, enabling users to efficiently discover, access, and apply geospatial evidence for decision-making.

The integration of these dashboards is essential for informed and effective planning, as land, soil, biodiversity, and climate systems are inherently interconnected. Policies, interventions, and management actions in one sector often generate direct and indirect impacts across others.

By strengthening coordination among government ministries, researchers, development partners, and implementing agencies, the portal reduces duplication of effort, promotes consistency in data interpretation, and enhances the use of shared evidence for policy formulation, project planning, monitoring, and reporting.

Rather than replacing individual systems, the portal preserves the full analytical depth and functionality of each dashboard while improving their visibility, interoperability, and usability.
