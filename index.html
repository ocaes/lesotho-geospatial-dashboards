Welcome

This portal provides unified access to key geospatial dashboards supporting land management, soil assessment, biodiversity monitoring, and climate-related analysis in Lesotho. It serves as a centralized gateway to spatial information systems developed by national institutions and partners, enabling users to efficiently discover, access, and apply geospatial evidence.

The integration of these dashboards is essential for informed decision-making, as land, soil, biodiversity, and climate systems are inherently interconnected. Policies, interventions, or management actions in one sector often have direct and indirect implications for others. Integrated access supports a holistic understanding of landscape dynamics and environmental change.

By strengthening coordination among government ministries, researchers, development partners, and implementing agencies, the portal reduces duplication of effort, promotes consistency in data interpretation, and enhances the use of shared evidence for policy formulation, project planning, monitoring, and reporting. This is particularly important for national priorities such as sustainable land management, climate resilience, ecosystem restoration, and biodiversity conservation.

Rather than replacing individual systems, the portal preserves the full analytical depth and functionality of each dashboard while improving visibility, interoperability, and usability. Users are encouraged to consult multiple dashboards collectively to support integrated planning, adaptive management, and evidence-based decision-making in the Kingdom of Lesotho.
